Crystals Collectors

Here's how the game works:

  1. When you start, the computer shows a random number. In order to guess it, you must click on the crystals which also have random values and are hidden.
  2. When you click on a crystal, a specific amount will appear and be added to the score.
  3. You win if your total score matches the random number. You lose if you go above it.
  4. The game restarts whenever you win or lose.
